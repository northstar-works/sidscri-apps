StreamVault is a personal media streaming organizer that lets you save, categorize, and play streaming URLs through an integrated video player.

Features:
• Add media with custom titles, URLs, and categories
• Organize your library with category filters and search
• Integrated full-screen video player with overlay controls
• Tap-to-show controls: back, previous, play/pause, next
• Skip through your entire library with prev/next navigation
• Persistent library — your collection survives app restarts
• Works with any streamable URL (iframe-compatible)
• Dark theme optimized for media consumption
• No accounts, no tracking, no ads — fully offline-capable library management

StreamVault is designed for users who want a simple, private way to organize and access their personal media streams without relying on third-party apps or services.

This app requires an active internet connection to stream media content. All library data is stored locally on your device.
